# How to Use Teacher Goals to Plan Support

If you have had your teachers self-reflect and/or set active goals for themselves in Bullseye, then it is key to use that information in your classroom visits. This allows you to provide personalized PD for your teachers through your coaching. Check out this video to see how you can use teacher goals to plan support.
