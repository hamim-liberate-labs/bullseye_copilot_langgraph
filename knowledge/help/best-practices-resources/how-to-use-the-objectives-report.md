# How To Use the Objectives Report

As an administrator, you can use the objectives report to inform both school-wide professional development and support individual needs.



As you look at this report you will notice where your staff has demonstrated success, and where you may want to plan some professional development.



You are also able to drill into each objective to give you a breakdown of scores and notes. You can sort by mastery to see who has demonstrated this skill effectively, as well as who may need more support.



This also gives you information to strategically pair teachers for peer visits, collaboration sessions, or you can ask a teacher to lead a professional development.



This report allows you to see school-wide trends but you can always drill deeper into the data to understand exactly what is happening throughout your school.
