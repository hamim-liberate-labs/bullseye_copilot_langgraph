# Quick Tips for Feedback

Administrators want to ensure their feedback is meaningful and has a positive impact on teachers and ultimately their students. Below is a video highlighting three quick tips for ensuring your feedback is productive.



1. Always provide evidence.

2. Identify only one or two areas for growth.

3. Leave objectives blank if they are not observed or the highest leverage piece of feedback.
