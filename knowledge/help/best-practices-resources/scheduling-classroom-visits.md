**Founder and Superintendent of Etoile Academy, Kayleigh Colombero,** blocks out time every day dedicated to walkthroughs and debrief conversations. Kayleigh’s belief is that if there is not time set aside for getting into, it may not happen and so it is incredibly important to put aside the time.

1. Block out time for coaching conversations with teachers (weekly or monthly). This should live on both the teacher and administrator’s calendar.

2. Block out an hour each day for walkthroughs

3. Plan out who you are going to observe using your school-wide calendar while trying to observe the portion of the lesson aligned to what the teacher is working on.

4. Schedule time to calibrate with other administrators to ensure your feedback is aligned
