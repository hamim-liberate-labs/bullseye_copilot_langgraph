# District User Overview

This video provides an overview of how district administrators can manage users across all schools in Bullseye. You’ll learn how to view district users, invite new staff, assign roles and school access, update user settings at individual schools, and deactivate users at the district level. This ensures staff roles and access stay accurate as assignments change.

[https://player.vimeo.com/video/1163733118](https://player.vimeo.com/video/1163733118)

You can also find a step by step guide on how to move staff from one school to another in the district [here](https://intercom.help/bullseye-education/en/articles/12985449-how-to-move-staff-from-one-school-to-another-in-a-district).
