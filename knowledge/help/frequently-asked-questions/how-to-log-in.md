# How to Log In

**If you've never logged into Bullseye before:**

1. Find the email from <no-reply@bullseye.com>

2. Click accept invitation or sign in with Google Single Sign On

3. Set your password if needed

4. Save Bullseye as a bookmark to your device

**If you've logged into Bullseye before:**

1. Go to [www.bullseyeedu.com](http://www.bullseyeedu.com)

2. Either log in with your username and password or click Sign in with Google

3. Save Bullseye as a bookmark to your device
