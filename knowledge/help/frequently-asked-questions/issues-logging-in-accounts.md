# Issues Logging In / Accounts

**Issues with Logging In (Admin Support for Staff)**

If your staff is having trouble logging into Bullseye, follow these steps to help them get back on track:

**1. Confirm Staff Setup in Bullseye**

First, make sure the staff member is set up correctly in the system.

- Go to School Admin > Staff.

- Search for the staff member's name.

- If their profile appears, click Edit and check that the email address is correct.

**2. Staff Member Not Found? Add Them!**

If the staff member doesn’t show up in the system, you’ll need to add them.

- Go to School Admin > Staff.

- Click Invite New Staff and fill in the required details.

- Click [HERE](https://intercom.help/bullseye-education/*/articles/12985447) for a video demonstration and how to properly enter all of the required details.

**3. Check Invite Status**

If the staff member's profile is showing but they still can't log in, check if they’ve accepted their invite.

- In the Staff section, search for the staff member’s name again.

- Look for a button labeled Resend Invite next to their name.

  - If the Resend Invite button is visible, click it to send a new invite.

  - If there’s no Resend Invite button, it means the staff member has already accepted the invitation. In this case, ask them to go to[www.bullseyeedu.com](https://www.bullseyeedu.com) and click Forgot Password to reset their password and regain access.

**4. Still Having Issues?**

If none of these steps work, reach out to our support team for further assistance!

**Issues with Logging In (User Support for Staff)**

**Not Receiving Welcome Emails or Other Bullseye Emails?**

If you or someone on your team is not seeing important emails from Bullseye (including your welcome email), try these steps:

**1. Check Your Spam/Junk Folder**

Emails from Bullseye might have been mistakenly flagged. If you find them there, mark them as “Looks Safe” to move them back to your inbox.

**2. Verify Your Email Address**

Ask an administrator on your admin team to ensure your email is entered correctly in the system.

- Go to School Admin > Staff > Edit Staff and confirm the email address is correct.

**3. Whitelist Bullseye Emails**

Ask your admin to whitelist the domain @bullseyeedu.com to ensure future emails come through without issue.

**Trouble Logging In?**

**1. Logging in with Google?**

If you’re using Single Sign-On (SSO) with Google and see a message that says “You need to sign in or sign up before continuing”, it means there is no account in Bullseye with that email address. Ask your admin to create an account for you.

**2. Logging in with a Username and Password?**

If you haven’t set up your account yet:

- Go to [www.bullseyeedu.com](http://www.bullseyeedu.com)

- Click “Didn’t Receive Confirmation Instructions?” to resend your welcome email and set your password.

If you need to reset your password:

- Click “Forgot Password” and check your inbox (including your spam folder) for an email with a reset link.

3. **If you see a message saying “There is no account with that email address”,** it means your admin needs to add you to the system. Please reach out to them for assistance.

Still Having Trouble?

If none of these steps work, reach out to our support team for further assistance!



**How to Reset Your Password**

1. If you need to reset your password:

Click “Forgot Password” and check your inbox (including your spam folder) for an email with a reset link.

2. If you see a message saying “There is no account with that email address”, it means your admin needs to add you to the system. Please reach out to them for assistance.

**Issue Adding a New User**

If you're unable to add a new user, here's what to check:

**1. Ensure all required fields are filled in**

Check that you’ve selected a role, added the correct first name, last name, and email address.

**2. Check If the User Already Exists**

If there is already an account associated with the email you're trying to use (even if it's deactivated), you won’t be able to add a new user with the same email address.

- Go to School Admin and search for the staff name to confirm the user doesn’t already exist

**3. Reactivate the Existing User**

If the user has a previous account in the system:

- Reactivate the existing user instead of creating a new one.

- Once reactivated, you can edit their details as needed (e.g., updating their role, email, etc.).

If none of these steps work, reach out to our support team for further assistance!

**Adding a User Who Was Previously at Another School?**

If you’re trying to add someone who was a user at another school, here's what to do:

**1. Add Them as a New User**

*[Link to: HERE](https://intercom.help/bullseye-education/*/articles/12985447)*

Add them to your school as you would any new staff member (click HERE for a video with directions for adding a new user). When they log into Bullseye they will be able to access the new school. To switch between schools, click on your name to open a dropdown of schools.

**2. Need to Remove Them from the Previous School?**

*[Link to: HERE](https://intercom.help/bullseye-education/*/articles/12985448)*

If the teacher needs to be removed from their previous school, you’ll need to ask an administrator at that school (or someone from the district office) to deactivate their account at that school. HERE is a video of how to remove a user.

This process ensures that the user is only active at one school at a time while still being able to join your team!
