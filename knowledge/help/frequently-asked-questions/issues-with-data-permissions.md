# Issues with Data Permissions

**Issue with Data Permissions**

**1. Observer Not Seeing a Learner When Launching a Session**

If you launch a session and don’t see the person you want to take a session on, it’s likely because the teacher/user has not been added to the Staff Group as a Learner.

Follow these steps to fix it:

1. Go to School Admin > Staff.

2. Find the user and click Edit.

3. Under Staff Group as Learner, make sure the user is added to the correct staff group.

4. Save your changes, and the user should now appear when launching the session.

**2. Staff Seeing Data for Other Staff When They Shouldn’t**

If people on your team are seeing data from other users when they shouldn’t, here’s what to check:

1. Go to School Admin > Staff.

2. Click Edit next to the user’s name.

3. Ensure they’re listed as a Learner (not an Observer) in the appropriate Staff Group.

4. If they are listed as an Observer of a Staff Group, remove the staff group from the section.

5. Make sure they do not have Session Data Access for any templates they shouldn't have visibility on for other users.

**3. Browser Loads the Previous School Year**

If Bullseye keeps directing you to the previous school year, it may be due to the school year being embedded in the URL, which your browser has saved.

Here’s how to switch to the current year:

1. Click on your name in the upper-right corner of the Bullseye interface.

2. From the dropdown, select the correct school year.

3. For easier access in the future, bookmark the current school year page.

**4. User Does Not Have Access to Launch a Template**

To grant a user access to launch a specific session template, follow these steps:

1. Go to the Menu > School Admin > Session Templates.

2. Find the template you want to update and click Edit.

3. Under the Shared Staff field, add the user who needs access.

4. If the user should also view session data from this template, ensure they are added to Session Data Access.

5. Scroll down and click Save Session Template.

**Still having trouble?**

If none of these steps work, reach out to our support team for further assistance!
