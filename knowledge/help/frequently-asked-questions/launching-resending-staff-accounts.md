# Launching/Resending Staff Accounts

**Launching Multiple New Staff Accounts:**

1. Go to School Admin

2. Go to Staff

3. Click Resend All Invites. This will send an invite to all staff who have yet to log in to their account.


**Resending Single Staff Account:**

1. Go to School Admin

2. Go to Staff

3. Check the staff member's email is correct. To do this, click Edit and check the email address. Update as needed.

4. If the email address is correct, click Resend Invite.

5. If this button doesn't exist, it means this staff member has already logged in. If this is the case, have them log in and click Forgot Password to reset their password.
