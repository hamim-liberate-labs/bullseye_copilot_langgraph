# Bullseye Office Hours Schedule

**Office Hours Schedule**

Bullseye Office Hours are informal, drop-in opportunities to ask questions, get help, and connect with Bullseye experts.

Together, we’ll learn about your specific context so that we can develop a plan to get you into classrooms and move the needle on relationship building and teacher growth.

Bullseye Office Hours occur every Wednesday from 12-1pm EST/9-10am PT. Please click the link [here](https://us02web.zoom.us/meeting/register/CNodAY_YQzO5GsEZsnyRUA) to register.
