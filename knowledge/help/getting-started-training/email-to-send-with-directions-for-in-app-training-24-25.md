# Email to Send With Directions for In-App Training 24-25

Below is an email you can copy and paste and send to your staff with directions for how to access training resources within Bullseye. This includes a video about how to log into Bullseye and how to access the knowledge base within the platform in case they need help!



Hi Team,



We are excited to leverage Bullseye to streamline our instructional support. To dive into the in application training please [watch this two-minute video.](https://vimeo.com/963480285/659264d56d?share=copy)



**Instructions to Log In:**



- If you haven't logged in yet: find an email in your inbox from <no-reply@bullseyeedu.com> and click Accept Invitation (please check you spam and junk mail)

- If you've already logged in: go to [www.bullseyeedu.com](http://www.bullseyeedu.com) and log in



Best,
