# Get Started with Bullseye SY26-27

### **IF YOU'RE ADMIN TEAM HAS ALREADY BEEN TRAINED, SKIP TO STEP 4!**

**Step 1: Plan Your Training Session**

- Schedule a 60–90 minute session with your admin or leadership team

- Ask participants to bring a device so they can log in and follow along

**Step 2: Log into Bullseye**

- New users

  - Ask all participants to check for an email from <no-reply@bullseyeedu.com>

  - Have them click “Accept Invitation” and complete login steps

- Returning users

  - Log in at:[www.bullseyeedu.com](http://www.bullseyeedu.com)

**Step 3: Complete Your Admin Training**

- Watch the [Training Directions Video](https://bullseye.education/bullseye-training-overview/)

- Log into Bullseye

- Click the green button (bottom-right corner) to open the Knowledge Base

- Open the training checklist

- Start with “Overview of Bullseye Basics”

- Watch each video and click “Next” to progress through the series

**Step 4: Train Your Teachers/Staff**

- Schedule a 30 minute session with your staff/teachers

- Open the [Teacher Training Presentation](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1A9eVizdvkiXuOkH4XmALsOM-VcDdnA4ZLcvPKy5gLFA/edit?usp=sharing)

- Make a copy and make adjustments as needed

- [Launch your staff accounts](https://intercom.help/bullseye-education/en/articles/12985393-launching-resending-staff-accounts) before the session (school admin > staff > resend all invites)
