# How to Prepare for Staff Training SY26-27

**Step 1: Review Training Presentation**

- Open the [Teacher Training Presentation](https://bullseye.education/bullseye-training-overview/)

- Make a copy and make adjustments as needed

**Step 2: Schedule Staff Training**

- Schedule a 30 minute session with your staff teachers

- Add the session to your school-wide professional development calendar

**Step 3: Make Sure All Staff Receive Their Accounts**

- [Launch your staff accounts](https://intercom.help/bullseye-education/en/articles/12985393-launching-resending-staff-accounts) on the day of the session

  - Go to School Admin > Staff > Resend All Invites

- Update rosters as needed
