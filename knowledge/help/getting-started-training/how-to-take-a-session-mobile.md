# How to Take a Session - Mobile

To **take a session on mobile**, watch the video, follow the step-by-step instructions below, or check out the one-pager at the bottom of the page.

[https://player.vimeo.com/video/1157359726](https://player.vimeo.com/video/1157359726)

**Instructions** on how to take a mobile session:

1. Launch a new session from your phone

  - Tap Launch New Session anytime you’re in classrooms or on the go.

2. Choose a template

  - Select the template you want to use (e.g., Mastery Template).

3. Select the teacher

  - Choose the teacher and tap Launch.

4. Enter session details

  - Add the same information available on desktop.

  - Include tags and next steps as needed.

5. Add overall session notes and media

  - Tap the middle icon to enter session notes.

  - Use the camera icon to upload photos or videos.

6. Review the instructional framework

  - View objectives listed below your notes.

  - Tap the ⓘ icon to review success criteria and align feedback.

7. Add objective-specific feedback

  - Tap Add Note to leave feedback tied to specific objectives.

  - If using a mastery template, add scores.

8. Complete checklists (if applicable)

  - Tap checklist items to mark what you observed.

9. Finish the session

  - Tap Next.

  - Choose Save & Exit to keep it as a draft, or Publish to share with the teacher.

10. Notify the teacher

  - Send a notification to email the teacher that the session is ready for review.
