# SY24-25 Administrator Training

**Milestone 1:** **Training for Administrators**

1. Complete the interactive training experience in Bullseye

  1. [**Watch this quick video for directions**](https://bullseye.education/sy24-25-bullseye-training-overview/) for the Bullseye training experience.

  2. [**Log into Bullseye**](https://bullseyeedu.freshdesk.com/a/solutions/articles/60000505360)

  3. Complete the checklists including:

    1. Admin guided tour

    2. Human and organizational systems

    3. Training for teachers
