# SY24-25 Directions for Teacher Training

**Synchronous Teacher Training (Our Strong Suggestion)**



We have found that when schools train their staff during a professional development session, teachers are more engaged with Bullseye. It provides them time to go through this quick training experience and allows you to be there as support as needed.

1. Schedule 30-45 minutes of professional development time to train your staff on Bullseye

2. Open the [**Teacher Training Presentation**](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/12KTcyr8CYmWZ-J8DQgHkm29s8r3lLzZ4cpgigYOEeRg/edit?usp=sharing)

  - Read through the presentation to prepare for PD

  - Make a copy of the presentation if you want to make any adjustments

3. [**Launch your staff accounts**](https://intercom.help/bullseye-education/*/articles/12985393)  right before the PD session so they receive the welcome email to set up their accounts. If they have used Bullseye before, they can simply [**log in**](https://bullseyeedu.freshdesk.com/a/solutions/articles/60000505360)

4. [**Send a follow up email with your staff**](https://bullseyeedu.freshdesk.com/a/solutions/articles/60001257936) so they have directions for accessing in application resources



**Asynchronous Teacher Training**



If you would prefer to have teachers complete the training asynchronously follow the steps below:

1. **[Launch your staff accounts](https://intercom.help/bullseye-education/*/articles/12985393)** to ensure that all teachers have access to their Bullseye accounts and can begin the training process.

2. [**Email your staff directions to complete in-app training**](https://bullseyeedu.freshdesk.com/a/solutions/articles/60001257936)
