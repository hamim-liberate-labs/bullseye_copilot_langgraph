# Troubleshooting Guide: Accessing User Flow on Bullseye

We want to ensure your experience with Bullseye and our training software, User Flow, is as seamless as possible. However, we understand that you might occasionally encounter some issues. In this article you will first find solutions for how to address some common challenges.

At the bottom, we have also included a **recording** of the full training so that you can dive in immediately without waiting for full in app experience.

Below, we've categorized these issues and provided solutions for each scenario:

**Scenario 1: User Flow is visible, but videos are not playing**

This could be due to restrictions set by your browser or network settings. Try the following solutions:

1. **Adjust your browser's privacy/security settings:** Some browsers may block certain types of content like videos by default. Check if your browser is blocking any content and adjust settings to allow Vimeo videos. Follow these steps based on your browser:

- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/4410268?hl=en&co=GENIE.Platform%3DDesktop)

- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204098)

**2. Check your network settings:** If you're accessing Bullseye from a school or work network, it might have filters preventing video streaming sites like Vimeo from loading. Ask your network administrator to whitelist bullseyeedu.com and vimeo.com.

**Scenario 2: User Flow is not visible** (when you log into Bullseye, you do not see the resource center on the right hand side of the screen)

This could be because your browser is blocking pop-ups, which affects User Flow's functionality. Here's what you can do:

1. **Review AdBlock settings:** Your browser may be identifying User Flow as an ad. Follow these instructions to adjust settings:

- [General Browser](https://brave.com/learn/turn-off-ad-blockers/)

- [Brave Browser](https://allthings.how/how-to-disable-ad-blocker-for-a-website-in-brave-browser/)

**2. Enable pop-ups in your current browser:** Browsers usually let you control the blocking of pop-ups. Ensure you've allowed pop-ups for the Bullseye website. Follow these steps based on your browser:

- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95472?sjid=13903400607461112952-NA)

- [Safari](https://support.apple.com/en-om/guide/safari/sfri40696/mac)

**3. Clear cache and hard reload:** At times, your browser's cache can cause display issues. Clearing the cache and performing a hard reload of the webpage may solve the problem. [Here's how to do it.](https://help.codehs.com/en/articles/4951972-how-to-clear-your-browser-cache-and-hard-refresh)

**4. Check your network settings**: Similar to Scenario 1, if you're accessing Bullseye from a school or work network, it might have filters that prevent User Flow from loading. Ask your network administrator to whitelist bullseyeedu.com and vimeo.com.


**Account Access:** If these solutions don't work, you may need to grant Bullseye's support team access to your account to investigate further.

We're always here to help! If you're still facing issues after trying these solutions, please don't hesitate to contact our support team at <support@bullseye.com>.
