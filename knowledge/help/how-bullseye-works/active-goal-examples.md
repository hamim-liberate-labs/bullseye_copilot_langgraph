# Active Goal Examples

Check out our Active Goal Examples' resource below for inspiration and guidance on setting effective goals. Whether you're an admin looking to support your teachers or a teacher aiming to enhance classroom practices, this resource provides practical examples to help you set clear, achievable goals.



**Admin Goal Examples:**



- By the end of each school day, I will visit 2 classrooms and provide specific, constructive feedback to the teachers to ensure they feel supported. I will track my visits and feedback provided aiming for 100% completion each week.

- I will conduct at least one classroom observation for each teacher by the last day of each month, providing individual feedback within 48 hours of the visit to ensure all teachers feel supported.

- I will schedule and hold individual meetings with each teacher I support by the end of every month to discuss their progress, concerns, and development goals. Meeting notes will be documented and shared to foster strong relationships and continuous improvement.



**Teacher Goal Examples:**



- By the end of the first semester, I will implement differentiated instruction in my classroom by incorporating at least three different instructional strategies (e.g., group work, individualized tasks, and hands-on activities) each week to address diverse learning needs and styles. Progress will be monitored through student feedback and performance assessments.

- I will design and implement at least one activity per week that encourages critical thinking, information analysis, and real-world application of knowledge. These activities will be integrated into the lesson plans and assessed through student reflections and project-based evaluations.

- I will enhance parent-teacher communication by sending bi-weekly updates via email or a digital platform, holding parent-teacher conferences at the end of each grading period, and engaging parents through at least one digital platform (e.g., class website or app). Feedback from parents will be collected to assess and improve engagement strategies.
