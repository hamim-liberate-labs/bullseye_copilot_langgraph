# Automated Notifcations

This video explains how automated notifications work in Bullseye and how to customize them based on your role. You’ll learn how to navigate to **School Admin → Notifications**, turn specific notifications on or off, and use the question mark icons to understand what each notification does. Notification settings can be updated at any time to match your preferences.
