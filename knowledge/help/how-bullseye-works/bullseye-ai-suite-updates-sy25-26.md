# Bullseye AI Suite Updates SY25-26

The Bullseye AI Suite introduces a set of tools designed to enhance efficiency and improve the quality of coaching and feedback. These features are integrated directly into the Bullseye platform and support you in the following ways:

- **Generate trends and analytics** across objectives, sessions, and teacher profiles to identify patterns and inform decision-making.

- **Capture sessions more efficiently** by reducing the need for extensive manual documentation.

- **Improve writing quality** through AI-powered clarity, structure, and tone suggestions.

- **Align feedback to objectives** to ensure consistency and relevance in all coaching interactions.

- **Receive automatic suggestions for next steps** to provide actionable guidance that supports teacher growth.

These features are designed to streamline your workflow and strengthen alignment between observations, feedback, and instructional goals.
