# Bullseye Protocols

Bullseye is excited to introduce Protocols - a powerful new way to create and manage workflows across your organization. Protocols function like “next steps,” but are designed for groups, allowing administrators to assign specific action items to teams or cohorts. This makes it easy to track progress, see who’s completed their tasks, and identify what’s still outstanding.



Check out the video below for an overview of protocols!

[https://player.vimeo.com/video/1163753853](https://player.vimeo.com/video/1163753853)

Additionally, learn how to create custom protocols in Bullseye to structure walkthroughs, goal updates, and teacher-facing activities like self-reflections. This video walks through selecting a protocol type, assigning observers and learners, setting session expectations, and choosing start/end dates. ***Note: Protocols must be enabled by Bullseye—contact your Customer Success Manager to get started.***
