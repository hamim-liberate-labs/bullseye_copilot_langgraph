# District Resource Center Overview

The District Resource Center in Bullseye gives districts and schools a dedicated space to organize and share professional development resources. With both the district and school resource centers, educators can easily add materials, access shared content, and incorporate resources to support growth across the system. Watch the video below to learn how to navigate the centers, add resources, and use them for professional development.
