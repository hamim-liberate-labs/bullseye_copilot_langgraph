# E-Signatures

Bullseye now supports e-signatures within sessions, making it simple to collect and track signatures directly in your workflow. With just a few clicks, participants can add their signature directly into a session. It is important to note that any updates made to the session will automatically clear old signatures to ensure everyone signs the most current version. Quick, secure, and seamless — e-signatures are built right into the platform to keep your process moving smoothly. Check out the video below to learn more!
