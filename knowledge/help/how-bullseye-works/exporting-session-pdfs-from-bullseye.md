# Exporting Session PDFs from Bullseye

Smarter, more flexible PDF exports are now available in Bullseye. You can download updated PDF reports from any individual session, export all sessions for a specific teacher, or even generate a full school-wide PDF—complete with filters for templates, date ranges, and learner roles. Your PDF is created instantly and delivered straight to your inbox, making it easier than ever to save, share, and stay organized.
