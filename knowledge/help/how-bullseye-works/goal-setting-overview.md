# Goal Setting Overview

As a teacher, setting a goal for yourself is a powerful way to bring clarity and focus to your practice. It helps ensure that the support you receive is aligned to what you believe is most impactful and relevant.

For administrators and coaches, goal setting provides visibility into instructional priorities and allows both teachers and coaches to create and track professional growth goals aligned to coaching support.

## **How many goals can you set?**

- One goal at a time

## **Who can set goals?**

- Both the teacher and the coach/ admin

- Teachers can set their own goal from their dashboard or on their profile.

- Admin can set a goal for a teacher by going to the menu, selecting people, then selecting the teacher name and "edit goal", and publish.


## **How do admin see goals?**

- Once a goal is set, it will appear at the top of every session conducted on that teacher, as the "Active Goal"

- You can also view goals by going to the menu, selecting people, and select a teachers' name.

## **What are Best Practices for Goal Setting?**

Best practices ensure goals are meaningful and manageable.

• Keep goals specific and actionable

• Align to school or district priorities

• Review goals regularly during coaching
