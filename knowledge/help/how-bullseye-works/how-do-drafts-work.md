# How Do Drafts Work?

When you start a session, it is automatically saved as a **Draft** and is visible only to you (the observer).

You can keep the session in **Draft** status while you finish your notes or make edits.

When your notes are complete and ready to share, **Publish** the session. Once published, the teacher will be able to view it.
