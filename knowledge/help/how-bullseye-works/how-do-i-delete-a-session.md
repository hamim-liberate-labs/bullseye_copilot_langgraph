# How Do I Delete a Session?

To **delete the session** you must be the observer who took the session.

Here's how you delete it:

1. Access your sessions by clicking "Sessions" in the sidebar menu

2. Find the intended session you wish to delete by browsing or using the search bar

3. Click the session name to open the session in a new page

4. Click "Revise Session" button

5. Click the pencil icon beside the session name

6. At the very bottom of the page, click "delete" to delete session

7. Click "OK" in response to "Are you sure you want to delete this session?"

8. This session has been deleted.
