# How Do I Edit a Session?

To **edit a session**, you must be the observer who took the session.


Follow the steps or watch the video below:

1. Access your sessions by clicking "Sessions" in the sidebar menu

2. Find the intended session you wish to edit by browsing or using the search bar

3. Click the session name to open the session in a new page

4. Click "Revise Session" button

5. Make the edits you wish to make. Once you finish revising session, click "Next" button and select "Publish"
