# How Do I Know How Many Times a Learner Has Been Observed?

Administrators can see a report across the school to understand the number of times a learner has been observed in the last week, month, and all time. To access this:

1. Click on the menu

2. Go to "Reports"

3. Click on "Frequency"

4. View by learner or by observer and sort the table to meet your needs

5. Use filters to adjust the view as needed
