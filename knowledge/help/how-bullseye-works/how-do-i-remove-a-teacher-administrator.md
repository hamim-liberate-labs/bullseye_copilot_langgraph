# How Do I Remove a Teacher / Administrator

Only school administrators are able to remove teachers/ administrators.

Here's how you do it:

**To remove one person:**

1. Select "School Admin" in the sidebar menu

2. Select "Staff" tab

3. Find the staff member you want to remove

4. Click "Edit"

5. Click "Deactivate"

**To remove multiple people at once:**

1. Select "School Admin" in the sidebar menu

2. Select "Staff" tab

3. Click the small check box next to each person you want to remove

4. Click the red "Deactivate" button in the upper right of the table
