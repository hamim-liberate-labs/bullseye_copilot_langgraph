# How Do I Upload Media From Mobile When I Took the Session On My Computer?

Want to take a session on your computer but easily upload media that has been taken on your phone? Once you have started the session on your computer, follow the steps below to add media from mobile:

1. Click into the session you're working on your dashboard.

2. Click revise / continue session.

3. Click on the camera icon and upload media from your phone.

4. When you finish the session, click "Next" and "Publish".
