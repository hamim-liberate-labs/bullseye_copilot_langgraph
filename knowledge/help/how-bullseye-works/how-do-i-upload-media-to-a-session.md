# How Do I Upload Media to a Session?

# To upload a picture or video to a session. Go step-by-step below or watch the video how-to beneath:

**On a Computer:**


1. Launch a new session

2. Click on Upload Media

3. Choose the file you'd like to upload

**On a Mobile Device:**

1. Launch a session

2. Select the camera icon

3. Take a photo/video or upload media
