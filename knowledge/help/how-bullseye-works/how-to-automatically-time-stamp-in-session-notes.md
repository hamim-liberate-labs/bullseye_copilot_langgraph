# How to Automatically Time Stamp in Session Notes

Taking session notes in Bullseye is now even easier with our improved automatic time-stamping feature. Start by selecting your preferred timestamp format and then enable the auto time-stamping. Every time you type enter, a timestamp will automatically appear making it simple to track observations in real-time. Need to add notes without timestamps? Just turn the auto feature off and continue typing. This enhancement helps you capture detailed, time-anchored evidence from classroom visits, grounding your feedback in what’s happening during the lesson.
