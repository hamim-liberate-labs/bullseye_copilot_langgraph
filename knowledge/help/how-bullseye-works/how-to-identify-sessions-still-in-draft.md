# How to Identify Sessions Still in Draft

You can now track which sessions are still in draft mode—even if you’re not the observer. This powerful feature ensures sessions get published, ensuring teachers receive timely feedback and coaching cycles are completed. With this update, you’ll gain greater visibility into your team’s progress and keep everything on track. Check out the video below to see it in action!
