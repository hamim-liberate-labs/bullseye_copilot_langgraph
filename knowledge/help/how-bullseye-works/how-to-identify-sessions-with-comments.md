# How to Identify Sessions with Comments

In Bullseye, you can now quickly identify sessions with comments, making it easier to track ongoing collaboration and conversations. On the sessions page, a new comments column highlights which sessions have active discussions. At a glance, you can see all comment exchanges on any session. Simply click the comment icon to view the conversation and continue collaborating with your team asynchronously—even beyond your walkthrough visits.
