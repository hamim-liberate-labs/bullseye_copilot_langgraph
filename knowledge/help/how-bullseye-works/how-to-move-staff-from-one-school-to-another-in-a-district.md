# How to Move Staff From One School to Another in a District

A staff member can be moved from one school to another so they can continue to use the same account.



STEP 1: Add the staff member to the new school

1. Log into Bullseye and go to the school where the teacher should be added

2. [Follow these steps to invite the staff member to this school](https://intercom.help/bullseye-education/*/articles/12985447)

3. Be sure their email address and name is correct - if not, this will create two different users



STEP 2: Remove the staff member from their previous school

1. If you are a district admin, go to the school where the teacher needs to be removed from. If you are not able to access that school, email the principal of that school to do step #2

2. [Follow these steps to deactivate the staff from this school](https://intercom.help/bullseye-education/*/articles/12985448)



If you run into an issue, email <support@bullseyeedu.com>

For more information, you can find a video on how to view district users, invite new staff, assign roles and school access, update user settings at individual schools, and deactivate users at the district level [here](https://intercom.help/bullseye-education/en/articles/13688977-district-user-overview).
