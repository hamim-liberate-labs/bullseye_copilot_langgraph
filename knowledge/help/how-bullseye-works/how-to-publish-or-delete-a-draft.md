# How to Publish or Delete a Draft

Once you create a session, it is saved as a draft. If you need to pause in the middle of a session, simply go back to your dashboard. Then when you're ready, you can either publish or delete the draft.



**To Publish:**

1. Click into the session from your dashboard or the sessions page

2. Click "Continue Session"

3. Add additional scores/notes

4. Publish

[https://player.vimeo.com/video/730047648](https://player.vimeo.com/video/730047648)

**To Delete:**

1. Click into the session from your dashboard or the sessions page

2. Click Continue Session

3. Click on the blue pencil icon in the upper left corner

4. Delete Session
