# Private Note Taking

Private notes gives observers more flexibility during walkthroughs and coaching sessions. You can now take notes that are only visible to you, allowing you to capture key insights, reminders, or next steps without sharing them in the final feedback.

Whether you're jotting down observations for follow-up discussions or keeping personal reflections, private notes make it easier to stay organized and focused.

Let us know if you want to try it out in your next session and we can turn it on for your school.
