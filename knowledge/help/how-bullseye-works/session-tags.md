# Session Tags

Take your session insights to the next level with customizable tags.

Now you can categorize your sessions with ease, whether it’s by subject, grade level, or any other relevant classroom component.

Tagging sessions allows you to seamlessly sort, filter, and analyze your data within the Objective Report.
