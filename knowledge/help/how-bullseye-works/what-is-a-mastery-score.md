# What is a Mastery score?

A **Mastery score** is a score assigned during an observation to indicate performance on a specific objective.

Bullseye uses scoring bands to evaluate mastery sessions, and these scoring bands are customizable to meet your needs.

Below are examples of scoring bands using a 5-point, 4-point, and 3-point scale.

![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/kfbx7tta/1888640178/e501a6e3bf34c19498fc5667a2e7/Screen+Shot+2025-12-16+at+4_34_13+PM.png?expires=1781709300&amp;signature=89f51230972a5cb01a8caa42965e78aa5724357c7646f7aa3c81de2fbfb142da&amp;req=dSgvHs96nYBYUfMW1HO4zbcePEb1BkPrTu1wOM4Qa18%2Fc6wn94qaM7mti0y7%0AvjHJm3l6f21I1NveL0Q%3D%0A)

The **pie chart symbol** to the left of the score-word represents the score and provides a quick visual reference as you review feedback.

Next to each objective, you’ll find an **“i” icon** that provides a brief description of the objective in a dropdown, ensuring the mastery score is assigned in clear context.
